Consent Form

This game is part of a research study conducted by Ankush (Ph.D. Scholar) under the guidance of Prof. Varun Dutt and Dr. K.V. Uday at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. The purpose of the research is to find out how an individual’s decision-making is influenced in the presence of various insurance models against landslides.

Procedures

Throughout the experiment, you will be asked to make a series of decisions to try and maximize your payoff through your choices. You will be asked to imagine that you are a resident in an Indian Himalayan town, where you need to make monthly investment decisions in order to mitigate landslides. You will be making investment decisions monthly until the program stops on its own.
